Our Academic Programs

Crèche

Our loving and stimulating crèche program for ages 0-2 focuses on foundational development, sensory exploration, and early socialization in a safe, nurturing environment. Qualified caregivers ensure your little one's earliest educational experiences are positive and enriching.

Age Range:

0-2 years

Termly Fees:

35,000 CFA

Nursery & Primary

Our comprehensive nursery (ages 3-5) and primary (ages 6-11) programs build strong academic foundations with emphasis on literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking. Through play-based learning transitioning to structured academics, children discover their potential in a supportive environment.

Age Range:

3-11 years

Termly Fees:

37,000 CFA

Junior Secondary

For ages 12-14, our comprehensive junior secondary program offers a broad curriculum that prepares students for the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE). Our experienced teachers provide specialized coaching to ensure JSS3 students excel in this important academic milestone with outstanding results.

Age Range:

12-14 years

Termly Fees:

58,000 CFA

Senior Secondary

Our senior program offers specialized tracks in sciences, arts, and commerce with intensive preparation for the West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE/WAEC). With a consistent record of outstanding results and university placements, our graduates excel academically and are prepared for global opportunities.

Age Range:

15-18 years

Termly Fees:

58,000 CFA

Why RISE? Exceptional Education, Outstanding Results

At RISE, we believe in providing a comprehensive educational experience that nurtures both academic excellence and character development. Our unique approach combines rigorous academics with creative expression, technological innovation, and cultural awareness to prepare students for success in our increasingly interconnected world.

Expert Faculty

Highly qualified teachers with international experience and specialized certifications in their respective fields.

STEM Education

Innovative curriculum integrating Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics to develop future innovators and problem-solvers.

School Portal

Advanced digital management system providing parents real-time updates on student progress, assignments, and activities.

Weekly Activities

Engaging programs including Friday Home Class Days, Sports Wednesdays, and various themed learning experiences.

Our balanced approach ensures students not only achieve academic excellence but also develop crucial life skills through structured activities and events. From STEM competitions to sports days, cultural festivals to community service projects, we create well-rounded individuals prepared for future success in any field they choose to pursue.
